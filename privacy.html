Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 14, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the My Season iOS app ("the app", "we", "us") handles your information. By using the app you agree to the practices described here.

1. Summary

My Season is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not operate a user account server, we do not run analytics or advertising, and we do not track you across apps or websites. The one time your data leaves your device is when a photo you choose is sent to Google's Gemini API to perform the color analysis you requested.

2. Information stored on your device

  • Account identifier. If you choose Sign in with Apple, the app stores the anonymized Apple user identifier, and the name and email you approve, in your device's local storage. This is used only to label your data and is never sent to a server we control. You may also use the app as a guest.
  • Profile & preferences. Age, height, footwear, and style preferences you enter are saved on your device to personalize recommendations.
  • Analysis history. Your past results, a small photo thumbnail, and generated wardrobes are saved on your device so you can revisit them. You can delete any entry at any time inside the app.

This information is stored locally using Apple's on-device storage and is removed if you delete the app.

3. Photos and AI analysis

When you ask for a color analysis, the photo you capture or select is downscaled and sent over an encrypted connection to the Google Gemini API for the sole purpose of determining your seasonal color type and generating wardrobe suggestions. We do not store your full-resolution photos on any server. Google processes the image to return a result; please review Google's Gemini API terms and Privacy Policy for how they handle data sent to their API. We do not use your photos to train any model.

4. What we do not collect

  • No advertising identifiers and no third-party advertising.
  • No analytics or behavioral tracking SDKs.
  • No location data.
  • No selling or sharing of personal information.

5. Children's privacy

My Season is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Your choices and rights

  • Delete individual analyses from the My Colors screen, or remove all data by deleting the app.
  • Use the app as a guest without Sign in with Apple.
  • Stop sending photos to the AI by simply not running a new analysis.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves. Material changes will be reflected here with a new "Last updated" date.
