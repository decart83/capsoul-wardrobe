Discover the colors that suit you

Capsoul Wardrobe is an iOS app that uses AI to analyze your natural coloring from a selfie and reveal your seasonal color type — Spring, Summer, Autumn, or Winter. From there it builds a personalized capsule wardrobe you can track, with a color palette, styling tips, and shopping links.

📸 Snap or upload

Take a selfie in natural light or choose a photo from your library.

🎨 AI color analysis

Get your season with a confidence score and a breakdown of skin, eye, and hair tones.

👗 Capsule wardrobe

Mark pieces as owned, wishlist, or skipped, and add items you already have.

🔒 Private by design

Your profile and history stay on your device. No tracking, no ads.
